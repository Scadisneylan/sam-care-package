Sam's Care Package

Tummy time tea

Worst part of laparascopy? Bloat.

During the procedure, they pump you full of gas. Drink a cup of this tea once or twice per day to help relieve and release the bloat.

Fun fact: the bloat froom the gas is actually what causes most of the pain in recovery.

Sleepy time tea

Second worst part? Feeling tired but wired.

Brew this tea to help unwind.

Make a cup of bone broth with this powder when you're not hungry but you need to eat.

Smooshed whole food balls

Snack on these balls (hehe) when you're hungry but don't want to make stuff or order stuff.

SenneSoft laxatives
